Technology is one of those obstacles

High-schoolers and college students are unable to afford the technology they need for their classes

Recently, more and more schools are requiring that students have laptops as technology becomes more integrated into their curriculums. Google leads this transformation and has in many ways took over the classrooms with their education apps, including Google Classroom and Gmail, and low-cost laptops called Chromebooks.

While Chromebooks are cheap and very affordable, high school and college students need laptops that can run programs that are required for their class. Also, since Chromebooks run on Chrome OS, without internet, those students might not be able to complete their homework and projects even though they have a laptop.
